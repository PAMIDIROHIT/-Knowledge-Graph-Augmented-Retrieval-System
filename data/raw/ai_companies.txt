OpenAI was founded in 2015 by Elon Musk, Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, Wojciech Zaremba, and John Schulman. The organization is headquartered in San Francisco, California. OpenAI's mission is to ensure that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity. Sam Altman serves as the Chief Executive Officer of OpenAI. Greg Brockman is the President and co-founder. Ilya Sutskever co-founded OpenAI and served as Chief Scientist before departing in 2024 to start Safe Superintelligence Inc.

OpenAI released GPT-4 in March 2023, a large multimodal model that accepts image and text inputs and produces text outputs. GPT-4 demonstrated human-level performance on various professional and academic benchmarks. OpenAI also released ChatGPT in November 2022, which became the fastest-growing consumer application in history, reaching 100 million users in two months. ChatGPT is powered by GPT-3.5 and later GPT-4 models.

OpenAI received a major investment from Microsoft in January 2023. Microsoft invested approximately $10 billion in OpenAI, extending their partnership that began in 2019. Microsoft integrated OpenAI technology into Azure OpenAI Service, Bing Search, and Microsoft 365 Copilot. Microsoft's CEO Satya Nadella described the partnership as transformative for the future of productivity software.

Anthropic is an AI safety company founded in 2021 by Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, Tom Brown, Chris Olah, Sam McCandlish, Jack Clark, and Jared Kaplan. Several founders previously worked at OpenAI. Anthropic is headquartered in San Francisco. Dario Amodei is the CEO of Anthropic and Daniela Amodei is the President. Anthropic raised over $7.3 billion in funding by 2024 from investors including Google, Amazon, and Spark Capital.

Anthropic developed Claude, a family of AI assistants. Claude 3 was released in March 2024 with three variants: Claude 3 Haiku (fastest), Claude 3 Sonnet (balanced), and Claude 3 Opus (most capable). Claude 3 Opus outperformed GPT-4 on several benchmarks including graduate-level reasoning and undergraduate-level knowledge tasks. Anthropic uses Constitutional AI training methodology to make Claude helpful, harmless, and honest.

Amazon Web Services invested $4 billion in Anthropic in 2023, making it one of the largest AI investments in history. Anthropic's models are available through Amazon Bedrock, AWS's managed AI service.

Google DeepMind is an AI research laboratory headquartered in London, England. It was formed in April 2023 through the merger of Google Brain and DeepMind. Demis Hassabis is the CEO and co-founder of Google DeepMind. Jeff Dean, formerly the head of Google Brain, serves as Chief Scientist at Google. Google DeepMind developed AlphaFold, a system that predicts protein structures with atomic accuracy. AlphaFold 2 solved a 50-year-old problem in biology. Google DeepMind released Gemini in December 2023, a multimodal AI model family. Gemini Ultra, the most capable variant, outperformed GPT-4 on the MMLU benchmark.

Meta AI Research is the artificial intelligence research division of Meta Platforms, formerly known as Facebook. Yann LeCun is Meta's Chief AI Scientist. Meta AI released LLaMA (Large Language Model Meta AI) in February 2023 as an open-source foundation model. LLaMA 2 was released in July 2023 in partnership with Microsoft and is available commercially. Llama 3, released in April 2024, achieved performance comparable to GPT-4 through Meta's scaling improvements. Meta AI also developed Segment Anything Model (SAM) for image segmentation.

xAI is an artificial intelligence company founded by Elon Musk in March 2023. xAI is headquartered in San Francisco, California. Elon Musk is the founder and CEO of xAI. The company developed Grok, a conversational AI model. Grok-1 was released in November 2023 as a 314-billion-parameter mixture-of-experts model. Grok was trained on data from X (formerly Twitter) which gives it real-time information capabilities. xAI raised $6 billion in May 2024 at a valuation of $24 billion.

Mistral AI is a French artificial intelligence company founded in April 2023 by Arthur Mensch, Guillaume Lample, and Timothée Lacroix. The founders previously worked at DeepMind and Meta. Mistral AI is headquartered in Paris, France. Mistral released the Mixtral 8x7B model in December 2023, a mixture-of-experts model that outperformed LLaMA 2 70B on most benchmarks. Mistral raised $415 million in a Series A round in June 2023. Mistral is backed by Andreessen Horowitz, Lightspeed Venture Partners, and General Catalyst.
